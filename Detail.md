🛡️ CyberArena — Gamified Cybersecurity Training

An interactive web & mobile application that trains users to recognise and respond to real-world cyber threats through scenario-based, decision-driven gameplay.

Presented by Pydimarri Hema Harshini — Team Unicorn — Gameathon 2026

🚨 The Problem
1. Human error is the weak link

90% of incidents involve human mistakes

Examples: clicking malicious links, responding to scams, oversharing sensitive data

2. Rising AI-enabled attacks

Deepfakes

Voice cloning

AI-crafted phishing

These attacks are more convincing and scalable

3. Boring training doesn’t work

Traditional awareness programs are passive

Hard to remember

Do not change user behavior

🎯 Objective

Build a usable, evidence-backed gamified app that trains users to detect:

Phishing attacks

Social engineering

AI scams

Using:

Decision-based simulations

Adaptive difficulty

Actionable safety steps

Core Learning Goals:

Recognise

Identify malicious messages and websites

Act

Make decisions and see consequences

Adapt

Difficulty adjusts based on user mistakes

🎮 Game Concept — Four Missions

Users progress through four immersive missions simulating real-world threats:

1. Phishing Detective

Identify malicious emails

Inspect headers

Choose safe actions

2. Social Engineering Simulator

Role-play calls and chats

Detect manipulation using emotions and authority

3. AI-Crime Lab

Spot deepfakes

Detect voice cloning

Identify AI-generated scams

4. Malware Escape Room

Contain cyber attacks

Prioritize response steps

Restore systems
