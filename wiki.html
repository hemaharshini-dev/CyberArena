🕵️ Phishing

Phishing is a type of Social EngineeringPsychological manipulation of people into performing actions or divulging confidential information. where an attacker sends a fraudulent message designed to trick a person into revealing sensitive information.

🔐 Cryptography

Modern defense relies on SHA-256A cryptographic hash function that creates a fixed-size 256-bit (32-byte) hash. Common in blockchain and security. and robust EntropyA measure of randomness or unpredictability in a password or encryption key..

Passwords should be stored as Hashed PasswordsA mathematical representation of a password, making it unreadable if the database is leaked..
