🎮 How to Play CyberArena

Welcome, Guardian! CyberArena is your training ground to become a cybersecurity expert.

🎯 Your Mission

  • Complete 9 interactive missions across Social Engineering, Technical Defense, and Tools & Knowledge
  • Earn XP for every correct decision — wrong answers cost you points
  • Climb from Novice → Specialist → Elite Guardian as you level up

⚡ Key Features

  • Adaptive Difficulty: Missions get harder as you rank up (faster timers, extra steps)
  • Real-World Cases: Learn from actual breaches like the $25M deepfake fraud and LinkedIn hack
  • Safety Protocols: Get actionable security tips after every mission
  • Global Leaderboard: Compete with other players for the top 5 spots

💡 Pro Tips

  • Hover over links to reveal the real URL before clicking
  • Check email headers by clicking the sender address
  • Use the 🧭 Tour button anytime to replay the guided walkthrough
  • Enable High Contrast mode for better readability

💾 Your progress is saved automatically. You can close the browser and come back anytime.
